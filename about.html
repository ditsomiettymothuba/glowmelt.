Our Story

From modest origins to a trusted skincare company.

Our Mission

At GlowMelt, everyone should feel good about themselves in our opinion. Established in 2016 by dermatologist Dr. Angelah James, our goal is to develop safe, sustainable, and effective natural skincare products that produce real results.

Although we have expanded from a tiny lab producing customized formaulas for customers to a well-known brand with a national reach, we have never forgotten our basic principles of purity and environmental responsibility.
